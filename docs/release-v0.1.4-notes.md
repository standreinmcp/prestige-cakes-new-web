# v0.1.4 — Pagina produsului

Product detail page with nutrition info, quantity selector, trust badges, and similar products.

## What's included

- `/produse/[slug]` in the `(catalog)` route group
- `ProductDetailView` with hero banner, image gallery area, add-to-cart CTA
- Static product detail data for four showcase products (`product-data.ts`)
- Similar products carousel at the bottom

## Route

`/produse/[slug]` (e.g. `/produse/tarte-cu-fructe`)
